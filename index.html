Welcome to Mao!

Our charity organisation helps stray cats around Europe to find a loving home and a caring family.

Hi! We are Mao, a charity organisation that helps cats around Europe. Our shelter in Iasi provides rescued cats all the attention and care they need to live a happy life. We started as a small team, motivated by creating a safe environment for cats. At the current moment we activate in multiple countries around Europe, and with help from caring people we can continue growing and doing our work.

Interested in our work? Please find more infomation in our about us page.
